Event Details

Objectives

The main objective of this charity run is to raise funds for underprivileged children in Vietnam. We aim to bring the community together in support of this noble cause while promoting a healthy lifestyle through running.

Theme

This year's theme is "Run for a Better Tomorrow". Together, we strive to make a positive impact on the future by contributing to the education and healthcare of underprivileged children in Vietnam.

Activities

  • Opening Ceremony: A warm welcome with live music and motivational speeches from special guests.
  • Main Run Event: Runners will participate in various race categories, including a 5K, Half-Marathon, and Full Marathon.
  • Kids Fun Run: A shorter race event designed specifically for children to encourage physical activity.
  • Charity Auction: Bid on exclusive items to further support the cause.
  • Award Ceremony: Celebrate the top finishers with medals and prizes.

Additional Information

Participants are encouraged to wear the official event t-shirt and bring their best energy to support this meaningful cause. There will be hydration stations, medical tents, and volunteers stationed throughout the event to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for all.

General Information

How to Reach the Event Venue

The event will take place at the SECC ( Saigon Exhibition and Convention Center), located at 799 Nguyen Van Linh - Tan Phu Ward, Dist. 7, Hochiminh City, Vietnam. The venue is easily accessible via public transportation and private vehicles.

  • By Bus: Bus routes 20, 72, and 86 stop near the SECC.
  • By Car: SECC has a large parking area available for all participants. Use Gate A for easy access.
  • By Motorbike/Taxi: Motorbike and taxi parking facilities are available close to the main entrance.

Parking Facilities

Parking is available for both cars and motorbikes. The venue has designated parking areas, including a multi-level car park located at the west side of the venue. Please follow the signs to reach the appropriate parking zone.

Special Entitlements for Participants

  • Event T-Shirt: Each participant will receive a high-quality event t-shirt upon registration.
  • Finisher's Medal: All finishers will be awarded a custom-designed medal at the finish line.
  • Refreshment Stations: Hydration and energy snack stations will be available throughout the race route.
  • Medical Assistance: Medical tents with trained staff will be present at various points in case of emergencies.

Event Exhibitors, Partners, and Sponsors

We are proud to collaborate with a variety of partners and sponsors who share our vision and commitment to making a difference. Throughout the event, there will be several booths showcasing products and services from our exhibitors.

  • Official Sponsors: Vinamilk, Techcombank, and Samsung.
  • Exhibitors: Visit booths from Nike, Garmin, and more to learn about the latest in health, fitness, and technology.
  • Event Partners: We are honored to be working with UNICEF and the Ministry of Health to bring you this exciting event.

Participation Information

How to Participate as a Runner

Joining the charity run as a runner is simple! Follow these steps:

  1. Register online through our registration form.
  2. Choose your race type: Marathon, Half-Marathon, or 5K.
  3. Pay the participation fee (see pricing below).
  4. Receive your runner kit (includes race bib, t-shirt, and timing chip).
  5. Show up on race day and enjoy the run for a great cause!

How to Participate as a Volunteer

Volunteering at the charity run is a rewarding way to contribute to the event's success. Volunteers assist with registration, hydration stations, route guidance, and more. To join as a volunteer:

  1. Fill out the volunteer application form.
  2. Select your preferred shift and role (e.g., setup crew, race-day assistance).
  3. Receive volunteer training before the event.
  4. Attend the event and help us make it a success!

How to Become a Sponsor

Support the charity run as a sponsor and help us achieve our fundraising goals. Sponsorship opportunities include:

  • Title Sponsor: Gain maximum exposure with your logo on all event materials, including t-shirts, banners, and our website.
  • Booth Sponsor: Rent a booth at the event to showcase your products or services to thousands of attendees.
  • In-kind Sponsor: Provide goods or services such as water, energy snacks, or medals in exchange for sponsor recognition.

To learn more about sponsorship packages and pricing, contact us at sponsor@charityrun.com.

Rules and Guidelines for Participants

  • All runners must wear their race bibs and timing chips throughout the event.
  • Participants must follow the designated route and adhere to marshals' instructions.
  • Participants are encouraged to bring their own water bottles to reduce plastic waste.
  • Participants under 18 must have parental consent to join.
  • Volunteers must attend mandatory pre-event training sessions.
  • All sponsorship booths must be set up by 7 AM on event day.
